Here's a high-level overview of how these entities could be related:

    -Users enroll in Courses.
    -Courses contain Lessons.
    -Lessons have associated Quizzes and Comments.
    -Quizzes have multiple Questions.
    -Questions have multiple Options.
    -Users can make Submissions for Quizzes.
    -Courses have Assignments, and Users submit their Assignment Submissions.
    -Users receive Notifications.
